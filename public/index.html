How AnomaCare Works

Our intent-centric platform automates the complex process of healthcare coordination, connecting those in need with those who can help through blockchain technology.

1

Declare Your Intent

Patients or families submit their medical needs through our simple, secure form. Specify your condition, location, funding requirements, and urgency.

Example: "I need a compatible kidney donor within Lagos, Nigeria + $15,000 for transplant within 30 days."
2

AI-Powered Matching

Our Anoma-powered solver network searches across medical registries, donor databases, and funding sources to find optimal matches.

Example: "System searches donor registries, charities, DAOs, and individual donors globally."
3

Community Response

Donors, healthcare providers, and organizations respond to matched intents. Multiple parties can contribute to fulfill complex medical needs.

Example: "10 donors pool $15,000 while compatible donor is found in nearby region."
4

Secure Execution

When all requirements are met, the blockchain automatically executes the coordinated transaction, ensuring transparency and trust.

Example: "Funds released to hospital, donor contacted, surgery scheduled - all parties notified."

Real-World Impact: Kidney Transplant Case Study

A father in Lagos needed a kidney transplant for his child. Through AnomaCare, the intent was matched with a compatible donor in Abuja and funding from 15 global donors, coordinating with Lagos University Teaching Hospital - all completed in 18 days.
